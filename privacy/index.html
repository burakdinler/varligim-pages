Varligim for iOS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Overview

Varligim is an iOS app for personal finance management on iPhone and iPad. The product is designed with a local-first approach: your financial records are intended to remain on your device by default unless a specific sync feature is explicitly enabled.

2. Data we process

  • Financial records you enter into the iOS app, such as accounts, transactions, budgets, bills, and subscriptions
  • Basic app state and preferences needed to operate the iOS experience
  • App Store subscription status needed to unlock premium access
  • Limited diagnostic information for crash handling and app stability when available

3. How the data is used

  • To display and manage your personal finance data inside the iOS app
  • To verify premium entitlement from App Store subscriptions
  • To improve reliability, performance, and bug fixes

Your information is not sold for advertising purposes.

4. Storage and security

  • Varligim is built around on-device storage by default
  • Network communication, when used, should be protected in transit
  • App Store billing is handled by Apple, not by the app directly

5. App Store subscriptions

Premium access is offered through Apple's App Store. Subscription pricing, renewal terms, and billing controls are managed through your Apple ID and App Store settings.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data. Requests and policy questions should be submitted through GitHub Issues.

Do not post personal, account, or financial information in public issues.

7. Changes to this policy

This page may be updated when the iOS app, subscription flow, or legal disclosures change. The latest version is always published at this URL.
