Varligim for iOS

Personal finance management for iPhone and iPad.

Varligim helps you track accounts, budgets, subscriptions, bills, goals, and other personal finance records on iOS. The app is designed around a local-first workflow, with App Store subscriptions used for premium access.

What the iOS app covers

Varligim for iOS is focused on day-to-day personal finance management on iPhone and iPad.

  • Account and balance tracking
  • Budget and goal planning
  • Bill and subscription reminders
  • Personal debt and installment tracking
  • Local-first data storage on the device

App Store details
